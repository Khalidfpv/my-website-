The Journey So Far

Spark — Seven years ago I soldered together my first racing quad in a dorm room. One throttle‑punch later and I was hooked on the freedom only FPV flight can give.

Craft — Since then I’ve built more than sixteen custom rigs — from cinewhoops weaving film‑set hallways to long‑range beasts that surf desert ridges, fine‑tuning every gram, ESC and prop.

Mission — Today I capture cars for Porsche, stream 1080 p live to TV crews and win global FPV races. My goal is simple: put audiences inside the moment while keeping every set safe and compliant.
